Sweet Justice

UnityC#3D ArtWIP

An action/mystery game set in a vibrant, sugary-treat–themed world that’s slowly slipping into chaos. You play as Officer Pinkglaze, a seasoned donut cop who thought he’d seen it all… until now.

This game is a work in progress but I am proud to show my current progess on it. I have been working on this game for a about 8 months now, what began as a school project has turned into a passion project that I intend to continue working on and eventually release. This project is a culmination of all the skills I have learned so far from Unity Development, 3D modeling, animation, texturing, and gameplay/game design principles.

Battle Arena

UnityLocal Co-opBlender

Battle Arena is my first 3D game and as well as my first local co-op game. Two players on opposing sides of the arena battle to reach the other player's base to score points. Each player has Defending bots that will attack the other player as they try to reach the base.

I made the player and bot models in Blender and added simple animations to them. I created the arena in Unity and used C# to program the game mechanics. this project was a good introduction to 3D game development, Unity's new input sytem and the workflow between creating models in Blender and importing them into Unity.

Hacky Sack Attack

MobileTouch ControlsPrototype

This project was born out of the desire to make a mobile game that was simple and fun to play. I wanted to make a game that could be played in short bursts, perfect for killing time while waiting in line or during a quick break. The concept is simple the player controls two shoes that kick the hacky sack to keep it airborne while also performing tricks. The prototype is functional and was a great learning experience for me in terms of mobile game development, I learned how to implement touch controls and optimize the game for mobile devices. I also learned about the importance of playtesting and iterating on game design to create a fun and engaging experience for players. I might someday take this personal project further adding in game tasks that the player must perform while keeping the hacky sack in the air, such as hitting targets or performing specific tricks.
