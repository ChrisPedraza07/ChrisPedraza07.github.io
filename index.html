About Me

Hi! My name is Chris Pedraza, and I'm a student at Boise State University pursuing a B.A. in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile. I specialize in 2D/3D art and animation for media and game development, with a passion for crafting engaging visual experiences across digital platforms like games, websites, apps, VR, and AR. I love injecting a sense of humor and whimsy into my work—whether it's designing quirky characters or creating playful animations. I’m skilled in Blender, where I can model, rig, and animate characters from scratch. I also have hands-on experience developing games in Unity using C#, including a solo-developed 2D platformer and a 3D local co-op game. In addition, I’ve collaborated on group projects focused on AR and VR, which strengthened my teamwork and creative problem-solving skills. Beyond game development, I’m proficient in HTML, Bootstrap, and CSS, allowing me to build clean, responsive web experiences that blend visual design with functionality. After graduation, I’m excited to jump into the creative industry—whether it’s in games, interactive media, or digital art—and continue telling stories through animation and design.
